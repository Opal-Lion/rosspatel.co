# Ross Patel — Research Profile

## Identity
- **Location:** London, UK
- **Pronouns:** They/them
- **Education:** Cass Business School
- **Social:** @rosswellpatel (Instagram, X/Twitter)
- **Self-description:** Artivist, advocate, strategist
- **DJ name:** Polyamoross

## Current Roles
- **LIVE Green Impact Consultant** — LIVE (UK live events trade body representing 4,000+ businesses and 40,000+ artists). Leads collaborative intersectional climate action initiatives connecting artists, event organisers, policymakers, academics, UK Government, and the United Nations.
- **Chief Strategy Officer / EVP Strategy** — UMA Entertainment Group (chaired by Harvey Goldsmith). Projects include EarthAid and HERO The App.
- **Council Member** — Global Artivism movement
- **Co-Chair** — Global Music Advisory Board at In Place of War
- **Booker** — Club Love
- **DJ** — performs as Polyamoross
- **Manager** — Remi Kabaka Jr.
- **EQUALIZE Project** — Research project redefining music education in Britain. Cross-sector collaboration led by Young Sounds UK, Black Music Research Unit (University of Westminster), in partnership with Punch and Bradford Music and Arts Service.

## Past Roles
- **Co-Founder & CEO** — Whole Entertainment (8 years). Diversified into full-suite talent management and consulting agency. Made the company climate positive through low-impact practices, initiatives, and carbon offset funding.
- **Co-Founder & Operations Director** — Sweetshop Media (London creative marketing agency). Clients: Napster, Nokia, Ellesse, GoTickety, Aphetor, Sam Branson, Oppo, Bondly/Forj.
- **Senior Manager** — Grade Management (global business development for talent)
- **Board Director** — Music Managers Forum (3-year term, representing 1,600+ members on climate action and social impact advocacy)

- Intersectional climate action
- Challenging perceived binaries
- Creating intentionally inclusive spaces centered on safety
